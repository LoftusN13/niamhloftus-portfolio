About Me

A junior software developer based in Galway, Ireland. I have always had a great love and interest in media and technology, a love which led me to study for and obtain a B.Sc. in Multimedia from Dublin City University.

Following my graduation from university I spent two years working and travelling in Canada; a truly unforgettable experience. Upon my return to Ireland, and the arrival of Covid-19 lockdowns, I chose to spend my time learning new skills. I enrolled in Code Institute's Software Development Diploma which allowed me to gain skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and Python.

I aim to use these skills, coupled with my extensive hospitality background, to gain entry into the tech world. I look forward to finding my place and discovering what I can accomplish with the right company.
